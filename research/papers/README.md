# Research Papers

This project was developed after reviewing multiple peer-reviewed publications from academic publishers including IEEE Xplore, Nature, MDPI, Springer, Elsevier, and other scholarly sources.

The original research papers are intentionally NOT included in this public repository due to copyright restrictions.

Readers interested in the referenced literature should obtain the papers directly from the official publishers or through institutional access.

A complete bibliography of the reviewed research is available in:

`research/references.bib`

Project summaries and research notes remain available in:

• `literature_review.md`
• `methodology_notes.md`
• `paper_summary.md`
